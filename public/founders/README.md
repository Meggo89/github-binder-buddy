# Founder portraits

The files in this folder (`founder-01.jpg` through `founder-24.jpg`) are consumed by `FoundersDrift.tsx` on the homepage.

**The files currently committed here are placeholders** (branded navy/gold SVG stubs rendered as JPG). Replace them in place with the 24 real founder portraits when ready.

Requirements:

- 24 files named `founder-01.jpg` through `founder-24.jpg`
- Portrait orientation, roughly 4:5 aspect
- JPEG, around 300×375 up to 600×750 is ideal (they render 150px wide)
- Faces near the top, subject on a reasonably clean background (the component applies a subtle grayscale + gradient overlay)

The filenames are the only contract; the component does not care about anything else. Drop the real images in and they take over.
